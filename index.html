About Rhalza

Hi, I'm Rhalza (Shane), a 28-year-old developer, music artist, and digital artist. This page is a casual showcase of some of my projects.

In web development, one of my ongoing projects is a 3D sandbox. I've designed it to be fully interactive in the browser, and while it runs on any device, it's definitely a better experience on a desktop. I've also created a chess game with a bot, which you can play against directly on its own page. I am also actively working on an Integrated Developer Environment (IDE), called RyxIDE. Once I manage to finish a stable and functional version of it, then I will make it public.

Besides web development, I also enjoy making music and creating digital art. I find that working on different kinds of projects keeps my creativity fresh across all of them. Everything you'll find here is a result of that process.

What you take away from my work is entirely up to you. I simply enjoy building things and putting them out there for others to see and experience.
